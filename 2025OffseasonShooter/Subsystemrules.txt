Order:
    ENUMS
    VARIABLES
    METHODS
        -Constructor(s) 
        -Periodic methods go just below constructor (ex. periodic, simulationPeriodic)
        -State related methods (ex. manageState, setState)
        -Set and get methods (ex. setShooterAngle)
        -Other methods
        -Case methods (share names with enum values)
